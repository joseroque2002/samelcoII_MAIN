Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a power outage?

You can report it through our "Report Issue" page, or call our emergency hotline numbers found on the Contact page.

What information do I need to report?

Please provide your account number, exact location, and a brief description of the issue (e.g., broken wire, no power, flickering lights).

Is the reporting system available 24/7?

Yes, our online reporting system is available 24/7. Our emergency teams are also on standby for urgent repairs.

How can I track my report?

Once submitted, you can check the status of your report by contacting our member services or checking for updates if provided.
